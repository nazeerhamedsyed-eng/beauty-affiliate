Cookie Policy

Information about how cookies and browser storage are utilized on our platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve performance, and provide information to site owners.

How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

GlowVault Direct uses a minimal set of cookies and local storage technologies to power our interactive features and fund our operations. We categorize these as follows:

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies & Storage

These are required for basic site functionality. Specifically:

  • Interactive Skin Quiz: We use your browser's local session memory to remember your quiz answers as you progress through the questions, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • User Interface State: Local storage is used to save settings like active filters and search states.

2. Affiliate Network Tracking Cookies

Our website displays direct manufacturer shopping links. When you click one of these outbound links, our affiliate network partners (such as Awin and Impact) place a temporary cookie on your browser.

  • Purpose: These cookies register that you visited the manufacturer's online store via GlowVault Direct. If you make a purchase, the merchant pays us a commission.
  • Data Protection: These cookies do not store any sensitive personal data (like names or credit card details) and typically expire automatically after 30 to 45 days.

3. Third-Party Analytics

We do not run intrusive tracking networks or share data with third-party advertising companies. Any statistics gathered regarding page visits are kept anonymous and secure.

How to Manage Cookies

You can choose to restrict, block, or delete cookies through your web browser settings at any time. However, disabling all cookies may prevent certain interactive features of our website (like the Skin Routine Quiz or Ingredient Decoder) from functioning correctly.
