An Honest Review of Charlotte Tilbury's Magic Cream

Charlotte Tilbury's Magic Cream has long been a staple in makeup artists' kits, and after testing it for 30 days, we understand why. It acts as an incredible hydrating primer, smoothing dry patches and leaving the skin with a luminous, dew-like glow.

Formula & Key Ingredients

The magic lies in the 'Magic 8' matrix of ingredients, including Hyaluronic Acid to bind moisture, Rosehip and Camellia Oils to nourish, and Vitamins C & E to brighten. However, it does contain Fragrance, which may irritate highly sensitive skin types.

Our Verdict

If you want a luxurious prep cream before foundation, this is the gold standard. For daily hydration without makeup, there are more affordable alternatives available in our dupe finder database.
