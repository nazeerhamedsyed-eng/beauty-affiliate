Privacy Policy & Affiliate Disclosure

Transparent information on how we collect data, protect your privacy, and fund our operations.

Affiliate Disclosure

GlowVault Direct is a professional beauty comparison and review portal. To keep this platform free of intrusive banner ads and 100% free for users, we utilize affiliate tracking links.

When you click on one of our manufacturer links (e.g. "Buy Direct From Manufacturer" or "Buy Dupe") and complete a purchase, we may receive a small financial commission from the partner network (such as Awin or Impact). **This commission is paid entirely by the brand/merchant and does not add any extra cost to your purchase.**

We only recommend brands and retailers that we review and believe to be authentic. Our reviews are compiled independently based on formulation audits, active ingredient analysis, and user reviews. We do not accept payment to rate a low-quality product highly.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy details how we handle the personal information collected on this site. Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us.

1. Information We Collect

When you participate in our **Interactive Skin Quiz**, we collect:

  • Your answers regarding skin type and primary concerns.
  • Your email address (if voluntarily submitted to unlock your skincare routine recommendations).

2. How We Use Your Information

The email address and quiz answers you submit are used solely to:

  • Provide you with your personalized skincare routine recommendations.
  • Send you curated newsletters containing ingredient updates and direct manufacturer discount codes.
We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your email address or quiz answers to third-party data brokers or advertisers.

3. Data Security

We store email leads securely. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your personal data during transmission and storage.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not run third-party advertising cookies. Our affiliate partners utilize standard, privacy-compliant tracking cookies to attribute commissions for sales generated through our outbound links. These cookies typically expire after 30 to 45 days.

5. Contacting Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request the removal of your email from our records, please contact us via our Contact Page.
